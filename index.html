Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

Do you repair at home?

Yes! We provide home visit repair services across Sri Lanka. Our technicians will come to your location to diagnose and fix your sewing machine. We serve Colombo, Kandy, Galle, Negombo, Matara and surrounding areas.

Which brands do you service?

We repair all major brands including Singer, Juki, Brother, and other domestic and industrial sewing machines. We carry original spare parts for all these brands and ensure quality repairs with our 90-day warranty.

How fast can you visit?

We offer same-day service in most cases. When you book through WhatsApp, we'll dispatch a technician within 90 minutes. For emergency repairs, we prioritize same-day visits and provide 24/7 service in major cities like Colombo and Kandy.

Do you provide warranty on repairs?

Yes! All our repairs come with a 90-day warranty. If the same issue occurs within 90 days, we'll fix it free of charge. This warranty covers both parts and labor, giving you peace of mind about your investment.
